About Me

I am an associate professor in ThingsLAB in Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I got both my B.Eng degree and M.Eng degree from Department of Microelectronic Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology in 2007 and 2009 respectively. Then I became a Ph.D student in Institute of Computing Technology supervised by Prof. Xiaowei Li, Prof. Yinhe Han and Prof. Lei Zhang. Afterwards, I transferred to Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at The University of Hong Kong in 2011 and got my Ph.D degree in 2016, advised by Prof. Hayden Kwok-Hay So and Prof. Ngai Wong. After graduation, I worked as a research fellow in Xtra group led by Prof. Bingsheng He in School of Computing, National University of Singapore from 2016 to 2018. Currently, my research interest includes reconfigurable computing, customized computing and FPGA-based hardware acceleration for domain specific applications like deep learning and graph processing.
