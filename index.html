Brief Bio.

I joined Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences in July, 2018. Before that, I worked as a research fellow in Xtra group led by Prof. Bingsheng He in School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS) from 2016 to 2018. I got both my B. E. and M. E. degree from Harbin Institute of Computing Technology (HIT) in 2007 and 2009 respectively and I was guided by Prof. Liyi Xiao. I got my Ph.D degree from The University of Hong Kong (HKU) supervised by Prof. Hayden Kwok-Hay So and Prof. Ngai Wong) in 2016.

 

Research Interests

I am now in IoT research group of ICT led by Prof. Lei Zhang. My research interests include FPGA reconfigurable computing and customized computing with focus on graph processing and neural networks. I am also interested in computer architecture and agile hardware design.

Research projects

FPGA for AI system (2018-)

FPGA overlays (2011-2016) (QuickDough)

Network-on-chip (NoC) (2009-2011)
