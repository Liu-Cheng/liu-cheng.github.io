  General

       I am an associate professor in ThingsLAB in Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I got my Ph.D in Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at The University of Hong Kong, in 2016, advised by Prof. Hayden Kwok-Hay So, and Prof. Ngai Wong.
