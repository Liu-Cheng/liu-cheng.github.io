About Me

I am an associate professor in ThingsLAB in Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. I got my Ph.D degree in Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at The University of Hong Kong in 2016, advised by Prof. Hayden Kwok-Hay So and Prof. Ngai Wong. Then I worked as a research fellow in Xtra group led by Prof. Bingsheng He in National University of Singapore.
